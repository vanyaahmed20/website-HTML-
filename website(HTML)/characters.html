DEMON LORD - "I will rule the Demon Lord inside me, if my is to rule the world then I will rule my fate."

Demon Lord wants to take control over the castle. By destroying the castle and killing the King he would succeed. The castle is being defended by guardian or commander who will become Hero by defending the King and the castle and by defeating the Demon Lord.






MINIONS - "We follow the orders of our Master."

Small baddies which are sent by their Boss to attack the castle. Their goal is to get inside or destroy the castle. They don't know about the Hero who would be defending the castle. They have to finish him and destroy castle.






HERO - "A hero is one who knows how to hang on one minute longer. I will fight till my last breath."

Hero aims to defend his castle, which all of a sudden comes under attack by the enemies. The boss of the enemies has send his minions to attack the castle. His goal is to defend the castle and kill the incoming minions.
